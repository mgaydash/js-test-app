FizzBuzz

When you click on this paragraph, you will be prompted to enter a number. You will get a list of numbers starting at 1 and ending at the number input. If the number is divisible by 3, it will be labeled "fizz" and colored yellow. If the number is divisible by 5, it will be labeled "buzz" and colored black. If the number if divisible by both 3 and 5, then it will be labeled "fizzbuzz" and colored red.
